My research

Experimental evidence has shown that Heavy Ion collisions at the Relativistic Heavy Ion Collider (RHIC) at Brookhaven National Laboratory, create hot and dense matter, dubbed the Quark-Gluon Plasma (QGP), that resembles what existed a millionth of a second after the beginning of the universe.

Using the Heavy Flavor Tracker upgrades (a state of the art high precision detector) for the STAR experiment, our research project aims to study the details of the behavior of the QGP and its connection to the phase diagram of the strong nuclear force and the origin of matter.

-- Mustafa Mustafa and Michael Lomnitz
