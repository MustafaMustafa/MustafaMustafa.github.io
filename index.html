Welcome to my home on the Interent.

My research Experimental evidence has shown that Heavy Ion collisions at the [Relativistic Heavy Ion Collider (RHIC)](https://www.bnl.gov/rhic/) at Brookhaven National Laboratory, create hot and dense matter, dubbed the Quark-Gluon Plasma (QGP), that resembles what existed a millionth of a second after the beginning of the universe.

Using the [Heavy Flavor Tracker](http://science.energy.gov/np/highlights/2015/np-2015-03-a/) upgrades (a state of the art high precision detector) for the [STAR experiment](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/STAR_detector), this dissertation aims to study the details of the behavior of the QGP and its connection to the phase diagram of the strong nuclear force and the origin of matter.
